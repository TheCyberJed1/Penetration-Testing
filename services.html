Our Services

Every engagement is scoped to your environment, tailored to your risk profile, and delivered with clear, actionable findings — not just a vulnerability list.

🌐

External Network Penetration Test

Simulate an outside attacker targeting your internet-facing infrastructure

We attack your publicly reachable systems — firewalls, VPNs, email gateways, web servers — the same way a real adversary would, from outside your perimeter.

Deliverables

  • Executive summary (business-language findings)
  • Technical findings report with CVSS scores
  • Step-by-step reproduction evidence (screenshots, logs)
  • Prioritized remediation roadmap
  • Live debrief call (1 hour)
  • Complimentary re-test of Critical/High findings (within 30 days)
⏱ Typical duration: 5–10 business days
🏢

Internal Network Penetration Test

Simulate an attacker already inside your network (insider threat / compromised endpoint)

Most breaches involve lateral movement after initial access. We simulate an attacker on your internal network, testing for privilege escalation, lateral movement, and path to domain compromise.

Deliverables

  • Full attack path narrative with screenshots
  • Active Directory attack path map
  • Privilege escalation findings with CVSS scores
  • Lateral movement simulation evidence
  • Prioritized remediation roadmap
  • Live debrief call (1 hour)
⏱ Typical duration: 5–15 business days
🕸

Web Application Penetration Test

Thorough manual and automated testing of your web apps and APIs

We manually test your web application for OWASP Top 10 vulnerabilities and beyond — including authentication flaws, injection attacks, broken authorization, and business logic abuse that automated scanners miss.

Deliverables

  • OWASP Top 10 coverage report
  • API endpoint inventory and findings
  • Authenticated and unauthenticated test results
  • Business logic flaw documentation
  • Developer-friendly remediation guidance
  • Live debrief call (1 hour)
⏱ Typical duration: 3–7 business days
📧

Social Engineering Assessment

Test your employees' resistance to phishing and pretexting attacks

The human element is the most consistently exploited attack vector. We simulate realistic phishing campaigns, vishing calls, and pretexting scenarios to measure your team's security awareness and identify training gaps.

Deliverables

  • Phishing campaign metrics (click rate, credential submission rate)
  • Department-level breakdown of results
  • Anonymized individual findings (no naming without consent)
  • Security awareness training recommendations
  • Repeat campaign option to measure improvement
⏱ Typical duration: 2–5 business days
🔍

Vulnerability Assessment

Comprehensive scanning and risk ranking without active exploitation

Not ready for a full penetration test? A vulnerability assessment gives you a baseline view of your attack surface with risk-ranked findings and remediation priorities — ideal for organizations beginning their security journey.

Deliverables

  • Authenticated scan results (internal and/or external)
  • Risk-ranked vulnerability report
  • False-positive validation by a human analyst
  • Remediation priority matrix
  • Executive summary for leadership
⏱ Typical duration: 2–4 business days
🎓

Security Awareness Training

Empower your team to be the last line of defense

We deliver engaging, practical security awareness sessions tailored to your industry and threat landscape — covering phishing recognition, password hygiene, safe remote work practices, and incident reporting.

Deliverables

  • Live or recorded training session (up to 2 hours)
  • Custom slide deck aligned to your policies
  • Employee quiz and knowledge-check materials
  • Post-training summary report
  • Certificate of completion for attendees
⏱ Typical duration: 1–3 business days
